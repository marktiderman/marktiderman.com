**Life=Pain**
The ache of goodbye.
Brokenness.
Friends who let you down.
A sick baby with little chance of survival.

**Life=Joy**
A sunflower field in full bloom.
Laughter.
Friends you can depend on.
A healthy baby with her whole life ahead of her.

**Joy=Pain**
